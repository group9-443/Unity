Instructions for the camera for B2Part 3
1. move the camera with WASD keys and scroll to move up and down
2. Toggle free look mode - Press the scroll wheel button on the mouse and enter free look mode where the direction you move the mouse is where the camera looks and the WASD move in the direction the camera is facing
3. Click a character once to select, Click another place on the map to set destination of selected character, right click to activate navigation to the destination. Click a character again to toggle walk and run while navigating
4. Currently set to navigate to a destination given to gameObject Free Camera, in its cameraFree.cs Script, when play button is pressed.
